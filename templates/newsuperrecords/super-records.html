    Super Records

    Super Records is an Australian accounting firm specialising in providing efficient and cost effective solutions to our clients. We are completely Australian owned and operated and have set up a branch office in India, which is the large scale mirror of our Australian accounting firm.

    By mirroring an Australian accounting firm in our office in India we are able to provide prices that are only possible with an offshore model – but still maintain the high level of quality and control you expect when dealing with any other Australian accounting firm.

    Our branch office only provides Australian accounting services and has been doing so since 2003, so you get the benefit of our many years of streamlining and process improvements which results in an efficient solution that will work for you. It also means that our staff know the ins and outs of Australian accounting standards, as that is all they do each day.

    Super Records does not compete with our professional service clients and exists to provide an independent platform to get the work done efficiently and in a cost effective manner. We are not associated with any financial institution nor do we receive referral fees from any source, so your own independence is not compromised when utilising our services.

    Our management team have over 20 years combined experience in accounting and business services and you can rest assured that all of our clients benefit from that experience and expertise.
